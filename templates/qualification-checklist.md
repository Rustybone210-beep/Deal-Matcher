# DealMatcher — Lead Qualification Checklist

## Before engaging (5 seconds):
- [ ] Real name or business name?
- [ ] Profile looks legitimate (not bot)?
- [ ] Has followers/connections?
- [ ] Has posted content?

## Initial contact (first DM exchange):
- [ ] What asset types do they work with?
- [ ] Role? (principal, broker, wholesaler, bird-dog)
- [ ] Price range?
- [ ] Markets?
- [ ] BUYER or SELLER (or both)?

## Qualification (before spending real time):
- [ ] Company name provided?
- [ ] Company exists in state records?
- [ ] LinkedIn profile matches?
- [ ] Done deals before?
- [ ] Price range realistic?
- [ ] Timeline makes sense?

## Before making introductions:
- [ ] Proof of funds received (for buyers)?
- [ ] Listing agreement or authorization (for sellers)?
- [ ] Facilitation agreement signed (2% fee)?
- [ ] Both parties briefed on terms?

## Red flags — walk away:
- No real name
- Brand new social accounts
- Won't provide company name
- Asks for money upfront
- Pressure to skip verification
- "I have a $500M deal" (probably BS)
- Won't do a phone call
- Only communicates at odd hours
- Generic copy-paste messages

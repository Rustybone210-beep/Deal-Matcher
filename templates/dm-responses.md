# DealMatcher — Instagram DM Response Templates

## When someone asks "What do you do?"
Thanks for reaching out! I run DealMatcher — an AI-powered platform that connects deal sellers with pre-qualified buyers. Whether it's real estate, businesses, or investment opportunities, we match based on criteria, budget, and market. What kind of deals are you working with?

## When someone asks "Do you have capital?" / "Do you have buyers?"
I connect sellers to pre-qualified buyers through AI matching. I have investors actively looking for opportunities right now. What asset types and price ranges are you working with? DM me the details and I'll run it through our matching engine.

## When someone has deals to sell
Great — here's what I need to get you matched:
1. Asset type (real estate, business, etc.)
2. Location/market
3. Asking price or price range
4. Brief description
5. Your role (owner, broker, wholesaler?)

Send those details and I'll have matches back to you within 24 hours.

## When someone wants to buy/invest
Perfect — to match you with the right opportunities:
1. What asset types are you looking for?
2. Price range / budget?
3. Target markets / locations?
4. Timeline to close?
5. Do you have proof of funds available?

## When someone is skeptical
Totally understand — here's how it works: DealMatcher uses AI to score and match deals against investor criteria. No upfront fees. I take a 2% facilitation fee only on closed transactions. What kind of deals are you working with? No commitment to try a match.
